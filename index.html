About me

I was born in San Diego in the 80's and raised in Los Angeles. Later I moved to Reno, Las Vegas, and Salt lake City. I moved to NYC in 2005 after graduating from Berklee College of Music in Boston with a degree in Music Production and Engineering.

Slate Raven

I am part of the Software Devolpement team for the Slate Raven MTi/MTX Touch Screen DAW Controller.

Mixed By Jamey Z

I work as a Recording/Mixing Engineer for many Billboard 100 chart topping artist. In 2013, three albums I worked on were nominated for grammy's

Live Music

I play keyboards with New York Funk Exchange and Brandon Neiderhaur Band. We have toured the northeast since 2007 and played over 400 shows
